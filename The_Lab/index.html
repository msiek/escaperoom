You have 30 minutes to solve the many different style of puzzles in Movie Quality sets to get out before the virus is released. This fast pace, non-scary adventure is geared for the whole family. Frequently Asked Questions

 
What is an escape game?

Real-life room escape games are brain-teaser activities where people find themselves in a realistic environment full of challenges. The only way out is teamwork.

How long does it take to finish the game?

It depends on your performance. Our game is shorter than normal so you have a maximum of 30 minutes to solve the puzzles.

What if I need a hint or clue?

Don't worry, our staff members will always be there to help. They will monitor your progress through a webcam and be in constant contact via walkie-talkies. (Not recorded)

Where is the Lab Escape Room located?

The Lab Escape Room can be found on the first floor of Nightmare Haunted House located at 1001 North Ocean Boulevard, Myrtle Beach, South Carolina, SC 29577.

Where can we park?

We are located on the Blvd in Myrtle Beach. There is ample parking all around our business especially right behind the Nightmare Haunted House building.

Should we make reservations?

Yes, it is encouraged, but walk-ins are welcomed. We use a reliable third-party booking system called "FareHarbor". You will be redirected to their site where you can select the date and times for your participation.

How many people can participate at a time?

Each escape room challenge has a limit of 6 participants.

Is this appropriate for children?

Yes. The Lab Escape Room is appropriate for children. Due to the complexity of the puzzles and situation, the escape room is ideal for older children.

Is the escape room scary?

Not at all! The Lab Escape Room is a non-scary escape room experience.
